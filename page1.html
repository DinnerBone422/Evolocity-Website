What is Aerodynamics?

Aerodynamics is the study of how air interacts and behaves with objects that are in motion. This field involves analysing the flow patterns and forces generated by the movement of air around various shapes and structures. The primary aim of aerodynamics is to comprehend and control these forces to enhance the efficiency, stability, and performance of vehicles, buildings, aircraft, and other objects that interact with the air.

What are the disadvantages of Aerodynamic Design?

Incorporating an aerodynamic design in a go-kart can bring advantages, but there are also drawbacks. It can be complex and costly to create an aerodynamic design, requiring trade-offs in the design process. Additionally, the success of the design depends on several factors.
