Tyres

Benifits of slick tires
The tyres used in the image above are slick tyres, which have no tread pattern. Slick tyres are oftern used for go karts becuase they provide better traction and better handling of the vehicle on dry and smooth surfaces like the ones found on go-kart tracks.

Disadvantages of slick tires
even though slick tires have many benifts for racing on smooth dry surfaces, Slick tires also have some Disadvantages. like Deformation, increased rolling risistance and Heat generation.

Traction

What Is Traction?
Traction refers to the ability of the tire to grip the roads surface.

Why do Slick tires have better traction?
Slick tires tend to have better traction on dry and smooth surfaces like asphalt. this is because Slick tires have a larger surface area in contact with the ground. This provides the go-kart with better grip and traction. Better grip and traction means the go-kart turns and brakes more efficently.

Handling

What is Handling?
Handling refers to the how well the driver can maintain control and maneuverability in a vehicle. Better handling also means that the go-kart responds to steering inputs more quickly.

Why do slick tires have better handling?
This is because Slick tires don't have any treads to interfere the way the kart moves.

what is rolling risistance?
Rolling Risstance referces to the force required to keep the tires rolling it is influenced by various factors such as tire consturcution, matireals and tread design. Less rolling risistance reults in faster acceletration.
