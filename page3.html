Tyres

The tyres used in the image above are slick tyres, which have no tread pattern. Slick tyres are oftern used for go karts becuase they provide better traction, better handling and reduces the rolling resistance on dry and smooth surfaces like the ones found on go-kart tracks.

Traction

What Is Traction.
Traction refers to the ability of the tire to grip the roads surface.

Why do Slick tires have better traction.
Slick tires tend to have better traction on dry and smooth surfaces like asphalt. this is because Slick tires have a larger surface area in contact with the ground. This provides the go-kart with better grip and traction, better grip and traction means the go-kart can turn and brake efficently.
