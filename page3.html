Tyres

What is a Slick Tire?

Slick tyres are tyres with smooth and threadless surfaces. They differ from regular tyres, which have tread patterns that offer traction in different road conditions. Slick tyres are also referred to as Track or racing tyres.

Benefits of slick tyres?

The picture above displays slick tyres that have no tread design. Slick tyres are commonly used in go-karts because they offer superior grip and handling on dry and even surfaces, such as those seen on go-kart tracks.

Disadvantages of slick tyres?

While slick tyres offer advantages for racing on smooth, dry surfaces, there are also some downsides to consider. These include deformation, increased rolling resistance, and heat generation.
